About Me

I've done much research in both the fitness and the nutritional aspects of welness and felt like my basic knowledge would be useful, so here we are. Nutrition

The Nutrition subpage will break down topics such as CICO (Calories In, Calories Out), BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate), TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure), and Macronutrients. It will also help answer the question “What Should I Eat?” This page aims to provide you with a basic understanding of nutrition, offering a solid starting point to build your foundational nutritional knowledge.
